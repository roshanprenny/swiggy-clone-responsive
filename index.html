Swiggy is Indian online food ordering and delivery platform. Founded in July 2014, Swiggy is based in Bangalore, and operates in 500 Indian cities, as of September 2021.[4][5] Apart from food delivery, Swiggy also provides on-demand grocery deliveries under the name Instamart and an instant package delivery service called Swiggy Genie.
